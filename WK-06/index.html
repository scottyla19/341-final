APOD is the BEST!

Astronomy Picture Of the Day (APOD) is a website maintained by NASA. The website has been running since 1995 and features a daily astronomy image as well as an explanation of the science occuring within the image. This site is pays homage to the amazing beauty witnessed and knowledge gained from the APOD site
