Basketball Definitions

Shell Drill
A basketball drill used to teach proper defensive fundamentals such as defensive rotations, communication, and blocking out.
Drop Step
An offensive basketball move typically used in the low post with the offender's back to the basket. It is a power move with a big powerful step back wards towards the basket and a power dribble.
45 Shooting Drill
9 different angles each with two shots, a three pointer and a mid-range jumpshot. Therefore each angle has 5 points for a total of 45 points. Players keep track of their score and need to be above a certain score.
